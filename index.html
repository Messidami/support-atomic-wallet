About Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows secure peer-to-peer transactions over the internet. It operates without a central authority or bank, making it a revolutionary way to store and transfer value globally. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned investor, understanding how Bitcoin works is essential for safe usage and investment.
